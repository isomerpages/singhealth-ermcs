## **Abstract Submission Eligibility:**

* Authors who submit the abstracts should be either the main author or co-author.

* Abstracts should be based on Risk Management-related research / innovation / work / process completed between 2025 to 2026.

* Should your poster be chosen as an award-winning poster, **up to 2 authors will be invited to go on stage to receive the award during the poster award ceremony on 15 September 2026**.

*  Organiser reserves the right not to accept submissions that do not comply with the requirements. **All decisions are final.**
